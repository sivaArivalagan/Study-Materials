------------------------------------------
ER diagram.
------------------------------------------
Purpose:
--------------------------------------------
An E-R Diagram shows a database’s entities
and the relationships among the entities in a
symbolic, visual way.
--------------------------------------------
Definition
---------------------------------------------
The E-R model is a detailed, logical representation of the data for an organisation or business area entity
--------------------------------------------
Topics:
--------------------------------------------
entity type
entity instance
attribute
strong entity
weak entity
multivalued attributes
derived attributes
Relationships.
  Unary (one to one, one to many, many to many)
  Binary(one to one, one to many, many to many)
  Ternary

cardinality constraint;

  Total participation
  Partial participation
--------------------------------------------
